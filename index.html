The Legend

Steal from the king. Reward the people.

In a chain ruled by whales and kings hoarding their treasure, LOOT flips the script. Every transaction pays a 1% toll, and that toll doesn't go to a team wallet or a king's vault. It goes back to you, the holders.

The contract collects the fee, swaps it, and streams WETH dividends directly to every eligible wallet. The longer and the more you hold, the bigger your cut of the loot. It's redistribution, on-chain, unstoppable, and completely automatic.
